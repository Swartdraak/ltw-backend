# **Luminaris TechWorks LLC — Brand Identity Guidelines**

A comprehensive branding package designed to unify the concepts of **Illumination** (clarity, insight, revealing threats) with **Technical Structure** (AI, networks, fortified systems).
The visual and verbal identity is **clean, intelligent, secure, and future-focused**.

---

## **1. Brand Philosophy & Style**

### **Core Concepts**

* **Intelligent Protection**
* **Illuminated Insight**
* **Future-Forward Structure**

### **Keywords**

Sleek · Secure · Autonomous · Radiant · Precise

### **Visual Metaphor**

**The Digital Aegis** — a shield that does not merely defend, but **illuminates its surroundings**, powered by a neural network of light.

---

## **2. Primary Logo — “The Digital Aegis”**

The Luminaris logo combines a futuristic symbol with a structured, corporate wordmark.

### **The Symbol**

* Stylized shield formed from **glowing interconnected nodes** and **light beams**.
* Within the shield, an abstract **“L” of light** ascends through a geometric neural lattice.
* Conveys **fortification**, **motion**, and **advanced intelligence**.

### **The Wordmark**

* Positioned to the right of the symbol.
* **LUMINARIS**: uppercase, bold, modern sans-serif.

  * The **A** features a subtle upward arrow/chevron, suggesting ascension.
* **TECHWORKS LLC**: smaller, medium weight, spaced slightly wider for a technical feel.

### **Color Treatment**

* **Symbol gradient**:
  Deep Space Blue → Electric Cyan (left → right).
* **Wordmark colors**:

  * Deep Space Blue (light backgrounds)
  * White (dark backgrounds)

---

## **3. Alternate Logo Variations**

### **A. Horizontal (Inline)**

Symbol + “LUMINARIS TECHWORKS LLC” on a single baseline.
**Use:** website headers, email signatures, letterheads.

### **B. Stacked (Vertical)**

Symbol centered above the wordmark.
**Use:** social icons, app splash screens, patches, merchandise.

### **C. Monochrome**

Gradient removed; solid shapes only.

* **Black/Deep Blue** for B/W documents
* **White (Reverse)** for dark backgrounds or video overlays

---

## **4. Color Palette**

Cool, calculated, electric. Blues = stability. Cyan = AI-energy + illumination.

| **Color Name**      | **Hex**   | **CMYK**       | **Usage**                                |
| ------------------- | --------- | -------------- | ---------------------------------------- |
| **Deep Space Blue** | `#0A192F` | 95, 83, 45, 45 | Primary brand color, text, backgrounds   |
| **Electric Cyan**   | `#00D4FF` | 65, 0, 0, 0    | Accent, highlights, “light source”       |
| **Mid-Sec Blue**    | `#114B75` | 95, 68, 22, 4  | Secondary elements, gradients            |
| **Circuit Silver**  | `#C0C0C8` | 25, 19, 19, 0  | Borders, secondary text, subtle patterns |
| **Pure White**      | `#FFFFFF` | 0, 0, 0, 0     | Negative space, reversed text            |

### **Color Usage Principle**

* **Deep Space Blue** dominates backgrounds.
* **Electric Cyan** is used sparingly to guide the eye and create focal points.

---

## **5. Typography Recommendations**

### **Primary Typeface: Inter**

Modern, geometric, highly readable.

* **Headers (H1, H2):** Inter Extra Bold / Black (uppercase for H1)
* **Subheaders (H3, H4):** Inter SemiBold
* **Body Copy:** Inter Regular (light BG) or Medium (dark BG)
* **Technical/Captions:** Inter Mono (for code/data)

### **Accent Typeface (Optional): Orbitron**

Used only for impactful futuristic moments.

**Example:** “99.9% SECURE” on a homepage graphic.

---

## **6. Iconography Style Guidelines**

Icons must feel like components of the main system.

* **Style:** Monoline / Outline
* **Stroke:** 2px consistent
* **Ends:** Squared technical terminals
* **Colors:**

  * Electric Cyan or White (dark BG)
  * Mid-Sec Blue (light BG)
* **Accents:** Small “data dots” or breaks to imply connectivity

### **Examples**

* Circuit-lined padlock
* Gear with a neural core
* Cloud with shield overlay

---

## **7. Visual Theme Elements**

### **A. Neural Lattice**

Low-opacity pattern of thin intersecting lines + dots.
**Use:** Deep Space Blue backgrounds, business card backs, subtle website textures.

### **B. Photon Beams**

Angled Electric Cyan light beams fading to transparency.
**Use:** Framing content, guiding viewer attention.

### **C. Geometric Masks**

Photography shaped in **tech-forward forms** (hexagons, trapezoids, shield-derived forms).
**Use:** Slides, social graphics, case studies.
